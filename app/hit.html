Relationship types explained

  • No relationship - the two entities do not affect each other.
  • Gives way to - Entity A waits and gives way for entity B to pass
  • Moves together as a group - Entity A and B move together. E.g. a child following its parents, or a couple walking together.
  • Avoids - Entity A diverts its path to avoid colliding with Entity B
